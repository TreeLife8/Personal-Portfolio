scientist with a love for the environment

Equipped with a Masters of Research in analytical chemistry, I got the opportunity to work for SGS, the world's leading testing, inspection & certification company, as an industrial problem-solver.

This experience was the perfect stepping stone for me to engage with multiple industries all over Australia & New Zealand. When I realised my passion for solving industry problems, I decided to focus on a cause that moved me and to start my own sustainability consulting company for hospitality, Earth Cycle Solutions!

coding is the future

Whilst working with restaurant owners to implement sustainable strategies for their team, it became clear that, if we wanted them to change their work habits, we would have to make it appealing to them.

And if we wanted those changes to last, we’d have to provide them with tools that were easy to use, accessible, and that allowed the managers to monitor their success. This is when I realised how fundamental technology was going to be for this project and decided to learn software development, in order to create an app for all the needs of Earth Cycle Solution’s customers.
